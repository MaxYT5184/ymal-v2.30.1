About Ymal

Our Mission

Ymal was created with one simple goal: to provide a search engine that respects your privacy. In a world where every search is tracked, analyzed, and monetized, we believe there should be an alternative.

We're building a search engine that:

  • Doesn't track you: No search history, no profiling
  • Doesn't use cookies: No tracking cookies or fingerprinting
  • Doesn't sell your data: Your searches stay with you
  • Provides clean results: No sponsored results mixed in

How It Works

Ymal aggregates search results from multiple sources while stripping away all tracking elements. When you search on Ymal:

  1. Your query is sent anonymously
  2. We fetch results from various search APIs
  3. We clean the results (remove tracking parameters)
  4. We display them in a clean, readable format
  5. We don't store your query or IP address
