Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 18, 2024

Our Cookie Philosophy

Ymal uses minimal cookies and never uses tracking cookies. We believe in respecting your privacy, which means we avoid unnecessary cookies and never use cookies to track you across the web.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

At Ymal, we minimize cookie usage and never use cookies for tracking or advertising purposes.

2. Cookies We Use

Ymal uses only essential cookies that are necessary for the website to function properly. We do not use any third-party tracking cookies.

Cookie Name Purpose Duration Type
session_id Maintains your session for the duration of your visit Session Essential
preferences Stores your display preferences (theme, language) 30 days Functional
consent Remembers your cookie consent preferences 1 year Essential

3. What We Don't Use

Unlike many other websites, Ymal does NOT use:

  • Tracking cookies: We don't track your browsing behavior
  • Advertising cookies: We don't serve personalized ads
  • Analytics cookies: We use privacy-focused analytics that don't use cookies
  • Third-party cookies: We don't allow third-party cookies on our site
  • Social media cookies: We don't embed social media widgets that track you

4. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in various ways:

Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or delete existing ones.

Cookie Consent

When you first visit Ymal, you can choose which cookies to accept.

Privacy Extensions

Use privacy-focused browser extensions to block unwanted cookies.

Note: Blocking all cookies may affect the functionality of our website. Essential cookies are required for the site to work properly.

5. Third-Party Services

Some third-party services we use may set their own cookies:

  • Payment processors: If you make a purchase (future feature)
  • Hosting services: Our hosting provider may set security cookies
  • Analytics: We use cookie-free analytics services

We choose third-party services that respect user privacy whenever possible.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this cookie policy occasionally. We will notify users of significant changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Showing a notification on our website
  • If possible, via email for registered users

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about our cookie policy:

  • Email: privacy@ymal.space
  • Contact Form: Visit our contact page
