General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) Compliance Statement

Our Commitment to GDPR

Ymal is fully committed to compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Our privacy-first approach aligns naturally with GDPR principles, making us a privacy-respecting choice for users in the EU and worldwide.

1. What is GDPR?

The General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) is a comprehensive data protection law that came into effect in the European Union on May 25, 2018. It strengthens the rights of individuals regarding their personal data and imposes strict rules on organizations that collect or process personal data.

Although Ymal is designed to collect minimal data, we fully comply with GDPR requirements for all users, regardless of their location.

2. Your GDPR Rights

Under GDPR, you have several important rights. Here's how Ymal respects each of them:

Right to Access

You can request confirmation of whether we process your data and access to that data.

Ymal: Since we don't store personal data, there's nothing to access.

Right to Erasure

You can request deletion of your personal data.

Ymal: We don't store personal data, so there's nothing to delete.

Right to Rectification

You can request correction of inaccurate personal data.

Ymal: We don't store personal data that could be inaccurate.

Right to Restriction

You can request restriction of processing of your data.

Ymal: We process minimal data only for essential functions.

Right to Data Portability

You can request your data in a structured, commonly used format.

Ymal: We don't store personal data that could be exported.

Right to Object

You can object to processing of your personal data.

Ymal: You can stop using our service at any time.

3. Data We Process

In accordance with GDPR's principle of data minimization, Ymal processes the absolute minimum amount of data necessary to provide our service:

Data Type Purpose Legal Basis Retention
Search Queries To provide search results Contractual necessity Not stored
IP Addresses Security and abuse prevention Legitimate interest 24 hours maximum
Technical Data Service optimization Legitimate interest 30 days anonymized

4. Data Protection Measures

Ymal implements several measures to ensure GDPR compliance and protect your data:

  • Data Minimization: We collect only what's absolutely necessary
  • Privacy by Design: Privacy is built into our architecture
  • Security Measures: Encryption, access controls, and regular audits
  • Data Protection Officer: Designated person responsible for GDPR compliance
  • Processor Agreements: All third-party processors are GDPR-compliant
  • Breach Notification: Procedures for notifying authorities and users of breaches

5. International Data Transfers

Ymal operates globally, but we take special care with international data transfers:

  • EU Data: Processed within the EU whenever possible
  • Adequacy Decisions: We only transfer data to countries with adequate protection
  • Safeguards: Standard Contractual Clauses where needed
  • Transparency: Clear information about where data is processed
