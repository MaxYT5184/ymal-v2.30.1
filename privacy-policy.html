Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 18, 2024

Our Core Promise

Ymal does not track you, store your searches, or collect personal information. Your privacy is not just a feature‚Äîit's our foundation.

1. No Data Collection

Unlike other search engines, Ymal is designed with privacy as the core principle. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from our users.

What We Don't Collect:

  • Search queries: Your searches are not logged or stored
  • IP addresses: We don't collect or store your IP address
  • Cookies: No tracking cookies are used
  • Location data: We don't track your location
  • User accounts: No registration or sign-in required
  • Search history: Your search history remains yours alone

2. How Search Works

When you use search.ymal.space, here's what happens:

  1. Your search query is sent to our servers anonymously
  2. We fetch results from multiple search APIs
  3. Results are cleaned (tracking parameters removed)
  4. Cleaned results are displayed to you
  5. The query is immediately discarded from our servers

Note: Third-party search APIs may have their own privacy policies. We choose APIs that respect user privacy whenever possible.

3. Third-Party Services

Ymal uses the following services to operate:

  • Search APIs: For fetching search results from various sources
  • Hosting Providers: To serve the website (no access to user data)
  • Analytics: Anonymous traffic data only (no personal information)

4. Advertisements

Ymal may display advertisements to support our free service. Our ad partners:

  • May use cookies for ad personalization (you can opt out)
  • Are served through privacy-respecting networks
  • Follow industry-standard privacy practices

You can control ad personalization through your browser settings or using ad blockers.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Search anonymously without being tracked
  • Use our service without creating an account
  • Clear your browser data at any time
  • Use ad blockers and privacy extensions
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. We will notify users of any material changes by:

  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page
  • Posting announcements on our website

Continued use of Ymal after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or our practices:

  • Email: privacy@ymal.space
  • Contact Form: Visit our contact page
