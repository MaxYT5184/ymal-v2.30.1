Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 1, 2024

1. No Data Collection

Ymal is designed to be privacy-first. We do not collect, store, or process any personal information from our users. This includes:

  • No search query logging
  • No IP address storage
  • No cookie usage for tracking
  • No user accounts or profiles
  • No location tracking
  • No device fingerprinting

2. How Search Works

When you use search.ymal.space:

  • Your search query is sent to third-party search APIs anonymously
  • We fetch results from these APIs
  • We display the results to you
  • The query is not stored on our servers

Note: Third-party APIs may log queries according to their own privacy policies.

3. Third-Party Services

Ymal uses the following third-party services:

  • Search APIs: For fetching search results
  • Hosting Providers: For serving the website
  • Analytics: Basic traffic analytics (anonymous)

4. Advertisements

We may display advertisements through Google AdSense. These ads:

  • May use cookies for ad personalization
  • Are served by Google, not directly by us
  • Follow Google's privacy policies

You can opt out of personalized ads through Google's Ad Settings.

5. Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at:

Email: privacy@ymal.space
